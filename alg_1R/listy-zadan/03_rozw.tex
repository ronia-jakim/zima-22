    \section*{ZAD 1. $H<G$, $G$ - cyclic $\implies$ $H$ - cyclic}

    We know that
    $$G=\{..., g^-k,...,g^0=3,...,g^k,...\}$$
    Now, because $H<G$, we know that for every $h\in H$, we have
    \begin{align*}
        h&\in G\\
        h^{-1}&\in G\\
        (\forall\;h'\in H)\;hh'&\in H
    \end{align*}
